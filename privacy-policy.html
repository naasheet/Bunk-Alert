Privacy Policy for Bunk Alert

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bunk Alert ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Bunk Alert mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, and user ID when you sign in with Google or email/password.
  • Attendance and timetable data: subjects, classes, attendance records, targets, and related in-app content you create.
  • Device and app data: app version, platform, notification token (FCM), and basic diagnostic data needed for reliability and sync.
  • Notification preferences: reminder settings and alert preferences.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core features such as attendance tracking, analytics, reminders, and cloud sync.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Send class reminders and attendance alerts (if enabled by you).
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and maintain service quality.

3. Cloud Services and Third Parties

The App uses Google Firebase services (such as Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Messaging, and Cloud Functions) to deliver features.

These providers may process data on our behalf. Their handling of personal data is governed by their own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security

4. Notifications

On supported Android and iOS versions, the App requests notification permission so it can send reminders and important attendance alerts. You can disable notifications anytime in your device settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers required to operate the App (for example, Firebase services) or when legally required.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active or as needed to provide the App. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, update, or correct account information.
  • Disable notifications from device settings.
  • Request deletion of your account data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
